# Publishing

Once you are ready to publish your custom app to a screen, you should:

1. Create your `config.xml` file if you haven't already by adhering to the [file format](./configuration.md).
2. Zip up all your files for your application. Ensure that your home page is named `index.html` and is in the root of your zip file.
3. Ensure your zip file has the extension `.wgt`.
4. Upload your application into Fusion Signage from the Manage section by using the **Add → Custom app** menu.
